Job Description:

Join AT&T and reimagine the communications and technologies that connect the world. Our Consumer Technology experience team is delivering innovative and reliable technology solutions to power differentiated, simplified customer experiences. Bring your bold ideas and fearless risk-taking to redefine connectivity and transform how the world shares stories and experiences that matter. When you step into a career with AT&T, you won’t just imagine the future-you’ll create it.


The Senior Specialist Cybersecurity will serve as a full-stack web developer with a particular emphasis on designing, developing, and testing cybersecurity-focused applications. You will be expected to have experience with Agile projects and with most of the technology stack: Frontend (Angular, Angular Material), Backend (Node, Express), Database (MySQL).

About the Job:

  • Collaborate with an Agile team of software developers to design, develop, and test cybersecurity-focused applications.

  • Utilize our technologies (Angular, Node.js, MySQL) to deliver user stories in the full stack.

  • Provide demonstrations of completed features to team members and key stakeholders.

  • Write secure code and remediate code vulnerabilities that appear in Static Application Security Testing (SAST) scans.

  • Debug and troubleshoot existing features with senior team members.

  • Maintain DevOps pipelines for deployments.

Qualifications:

  • Required Bachelor's degree in Information Systems, Engineering, or Cyber Security related.

  • Requires 2-3 years of related experience.

  • Web Development experience, preferably with Angular as frontend framework.

  • Familiarity with JavaScript, Python, and Java.

  • Knowledge of vulnerability scanning tools for SAST and SCA, such as Veracode.

  • Strong problem-solving skills, and willingness to learn new technologies.

  • Past experience or interest in cybersecurity, in areas such as security policy and security awareness.

  • Experience with cloud service providers, such as Azure or AWS is a plus.

  • Experience incorporating testing and security scanning into a CI/CD pipeline is a plus.

  • Requires on site presence (3-5) days a week at the listed location. No relocation assistance is provided.

Our Sr. Specialist Cybersecurity earns between $94,300.00 - $158,400.00USD Annual, Not to mention all the other amazing rewards that working at AT&T offers. Individual starting salary within this range may depend on geography, experience, expertise, and education/training.
